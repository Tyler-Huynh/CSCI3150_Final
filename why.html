Why is Accessibility Important?

Universal Design is important because it allows for us to promote inclusivity when designing websites and other things that people use everyday. There are many aspects of Universal Design that are implemented to accomadate everyone regardless of what disability they may have, whether it be there age, size, or disability. Universal Design is here to allow for everyone the ability to live life as they see fit.

There are many reasons as to why implementing Universal Design benefits us all, some of them are below!

Save you Money!

Universal Design allows for less of a need when retrofitting a website to allow for inclusivity, thus making the decision to make the site inclusive allows for cost-effectiveness.

Inclusivity!

Universal Design allows for people of all abilities to have equal access and ease of use to innovative technologies and devices.

Benefits a Large Portion of Our World!

There is an estimated 1.3 billion people in the world today that experience a significant disability. Where this number is growing due to noncommunicable diseases and people living longer.

From this we can see that Universal Design is important to promote and to inlcude within our design of various technologies. Some of these disabilities such as visual impairments, mental impairments, mobility impairments, etc. it is up to us as engineers and designers to engineer and design tools that can accomadate everyone.
