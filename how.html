Importance of Universal Design

How can we help?

We can help implement and make Universal Design more widely known by just simply talking about it and implementing it into various designs that we do.

By doing this we can allow for Universal Design to take a stronger hold in more site and aspects of design.

Ways we can Help

There are many ways that we can help spread more awareness about Universal Design, some of those ways are listed below.

Education and Awareness

By providing classes on Universal Design anyone of any background can learn more about implementing proper Universal Design within their work.

Creating Laws and Regulations

We can also promote Universal Design by enforcing it within our laws, regulating companies and individuals to design with human intention in mind. Creating laws and regulations that are made so that people must abide by these guidlines to promote inclusivity.

Leadership

This will probably be the most important topic, as leaders within our world and industry leaders such as Apple or Windows can promote inclusivity within their own products to allow for others to follow within their footsteps. An example of this can been seen within many of Apple's devices, that allow for everyone to use their devices regardless of what disability they may have.

Listening To Users

Many companies already do this to better their products, but the ability to listen to what consumers are saying about their products they can better implement solutions that are designed to solve the issue and thus creating a more universally acceptable design that can be used by everyone.
