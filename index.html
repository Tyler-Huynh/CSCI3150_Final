Importance of Universal Design

History of Universal Design

Hello, and welcome to my page. This website is here to provide designers such as yourself more information as to what Universal Design is and the importance of it.

On the other pages of the site will be information to provide more context on Universal Design and how we as a society can benefit from the implementation and maintenance of it.

Origins

Universal Design is a topic that was coined by Ronald Mace who wanted to focus on accessible housing with a universal design.

Mace championed accessible building codes and standards within the United States, from this spawned Universal Designed being implemented in every aspect of society.

Univesal Design has evolved over the years, as in the beginning there were no laws to accomadate for Universal Design, as the century went on more laws were put into place gaining momentum and spawning better accessible practices within design.

Assistive Technologies also evolved during this time allowing for those with specific disabilities to be able to access everyday technologies and devices.

Due to this increasing rise in accessible design, design that was not originally made to focus on humans, was changed to then include the inclusivity of human needs even in the beginning of the design process.
